FAQ

If you have any additional questions, you can find them here

  • How do I choose the right subscription plan for me?

    If you enjoy trying different popular varieties and consume around 300g of coffee per month, the Standard “Espresso” Plan is perfect for you. If you are looking for larger quantities and unique and rare varieties of coffee, the Premium “Coffee Lover” Plan is a better choice. Our taste test will help you decide.

  • Can I pause or cancel my subscription?

    Yes, you can pause or cancel your subscription at any time. Simply log in to your account and manage your subscription preferences.

  • How fresh is the coffee I receive?

    Our coffee is roasted in small batches and shipped directly to you to ensure maximum freshness and flavor. You can expect to receive freshly roasted coffee, always full of aroma and flavor.

  • What if I don’t like the coffee I receive?

    We want you to love every cup of coffee from Coffee Haven. If you are not satisfied with your coffee, please contact us and we will do our best to resolve the issue, including replacing the coffee.

  • Do you offer gift subscriptions?

    Yes, we offer gift subscriptions. It’s a great way to surprise someone special with high-quality coffee delivered straight to their door.
